Delicious Chocolate Cake Recipe

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. In another bowl, mix together milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Remove from oven and let it cool completely before serving.
